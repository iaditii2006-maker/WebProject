The Challenge: Problems with the Old Website

Outdated Visual Design

The original website used outdated design patterns, dated color schemes (beige & brown tones from 2008), and generic stock photos. It felt impersonal and didn't reflect the boutique luxury experience the hotel offers.

Impact: 40% of visitors bounced immediately, trust scores were low.

No Clear Booking CTA Above Fold

Visitors had to scroll extensively before finding a booking option. The "Book Now" button was buried below 3+ sections of content, leading to lost conversion opportunities.

Impact: 60% lower direct bookings compared to competitors.

Poor Mobile Experience

The website was not mobile-optimized. Text was unreadable on mobile, buttons were impossible to click, and the booking flow was broken. 45% of traffic came from mobile but conversion was near zero.

Impact: Lost 60% of potential mobile bookings.
