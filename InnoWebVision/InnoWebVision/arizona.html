The Challenge: Problems with the Old Website

degrees Extremely Outdated Layout

The website hadn't been updated since 2004. It used Flash animations, Java applets, and layout tables. The design was cluttered, confusing, and didn't function properly in modern browsers.

Impact: 75% of visitors couldn't navigate properly, frustration led to immediate exits.

📱 Zero Mobile Optimization

The site was completely broken on mobile devices. Text was unreadable, images didn't load properly, and forms were impossible to fill out on a smartphone screen.

Impact: 80% of highway motel bookings come from mobile - they were losing nearly all mobile traffic.

📦 Complete OTA Dependency

With no functional direct booking system, guests were forced to book through Expedia, Booking.com, and other OTAs. This meant 15-30% commission losses and no direct customer relationships.

Impact: Thousands of dollars lost annually in commissions.

The Solution: What We Changed

Fast-Loading, Clean UI

Complete rebuild with modern technology:

  • Removed all Flash and Java (broken in modern browsers)
  • Clean, modern design using HTML5 & CSS3
  • Minimalist layout - easy to navigate
  • Page loads in under 2 seconds

Result: 90% faster loading, zero compatibility issues.

📱 Click-to-Call & Mobile Booking

Mobile-first design with direct booking:

  • One-click phone call button (tap to call)
  • Mobile-optimized booking form
  • Simplified 3-step checkout
  • Support for all payment methods

Result: 400% increase in mobile bookings in first 3 months.

💰 Clear Pricing Visibility

Transparent, trust-building pricing:

  • Room rates displayed prominently
  • No hidden fees (clearly stated)
  • Seasonal pricing transparency
  • Special offers & discounts highlighted

Result: 45% of direct bookings, reduced OTA dependency.
