The Challenge: Problems with the Old Website

Generic Design with Weak Trust Signals

The website had a completely generic design that could belong to any hotel. No unique branding, no personality, no differentiators. Stock photos looked fake, and there were no testimonials or reviews to build trust.

Impact: Guests couldn't remember the hotel or see what made it special vs competitors.

Complex Booking Steps

The booking funnel required 7+ steps with confusing field requirements, unnecessary information requests, and unclear pricing at checkout. Guests abandoned bookings 65% of the way through the process.

Impact: Only 3 out of 100 visitors completed bookings. Industry average is 15-20.

No Room Comparison

Rooms were displayed in a confusing list format with minimal photos. No side-by-side comparison, no amenity details, no way for guests to understand the differences between room types and make informed decisions.

Impact: Guests booked lower-priced rooms, leaving money on the table.

Conversion-Focused Homepage
